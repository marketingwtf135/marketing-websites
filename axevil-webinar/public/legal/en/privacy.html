Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Axevil Capital, LLC (“Axevil”, “we”, “us”) processes personal data collected through this webinar registration website (the “Site”) and via api.axevil.io. It applies to website visitors, webinar registrants and people who contact us through forms or email.

Short version. We use the data you submit on the registration form (name, email, phone, company, role) to confirm your attendance, deliver the webinar materials, contact you about related Axevil events and services, and to measure the performance of our advertising. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Who is the data controller

The data controller for personal data processed via this Site is Axevil Capital, LLC, a company registered in the State of Delaware, United States (registration number 6832739), with legal address at 548 Market Street, San Francisco, California 94104, United States. Axevil Capital, LLC holds Exempt Reporting Adviser (ERA) status and is regulated by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC #802-126907); it is also a FINRA member (CRD #323970). For any privacy enquiries please contact us at info@axevil.com.

2. What data we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Full name.
  • Business email address.
  • Phone number (including country code).
  • Company / family office name (optional).
  • Professional role (optional).
  • Anything else you voluntarily share when you contact us or talk to us in our Telegram bot.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Technical data: IP address, browser type and version, device type, operating system, screen size, time zone.
  • Usage data: pages viewed, time spent, scroll depth, referring URL, UTM parameters.
  • Advertising identifiers passed from ad platforms: gclid, gbraid, wbraid, fbclid, fbc, fbp (only when you arrive from an ad and where allowed by your consent settings).
  • Cookies and similar technologies — see our Cookie Policy.

3. Why we use your data (legal bases)

  • To register you for the webinar and deliver it — performance of a contract / pre-contractual steps you requested.
  • To send you the recording and follow-up materials — performance of a contract and our legitimate interest in keeping registrants informed.
  • To send marketing about Axevil products and events — your consent, which you can withdraw at any time using the unsubscribe link in every email.
  • To measure advertising performance and attribute conversions — your consent (for non-essential analytics and advertising cookies) and our legitimate interest in running and improving our marketing.
  • To prevent fraud, spam and abuse — our legitimate interest in operating a secure Site.
  • To comply with legal, accounting and regulatory obligations — legal obligation.

4. Who we share your data with

We share personal data only with service providers acting on our behalf and only as needed to deliver the services described above. Typical categories of recipients include:

  • Hosting and CDN providers (e.g. Cloudflare).
  • Email and CRM providers used to deliver webinar confirmations and follow-ups.
  • Webinar platform (Zoom or equivalent) to host the live event.
  • Analytics and advertising platforms (Google, Meta, LinkedIn, Telegram) where you have given consent.
  • Professional advisors (lawyers, auditors) where strictly necessary, and authorities where required by law.

We do not sell your personal data.

5. International transfers

Some of our service providers are located outside the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom or Switzerland. Where this is the case, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission) to make sure your data continues to be protected.

6. How long we keep your data

  • Webinar registration data: up to 24 months after your last interaction with us.
  • Marketing consents and unsubscribe records: for as long as needed to honour your choices.
  • Records required by tax or other laws: for the period required by the applicable law.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Have inaccurate data corrected.
  • Have your data erased (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Receive your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please email info@axevil.com. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

8. Security

We use technical and organisational measures designed to protect your data — including encryption in transit (HTTPS), access controls, and limited retention. No system is 100% secure, but we work to keep risk low.

9. Children

This Site is intended for professional investors, wealth managers and family offices, and is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of the page reflects the latest version. Material changes will be communicated through the Site or by email where appropriate.
